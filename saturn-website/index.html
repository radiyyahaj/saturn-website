SATURN

who we are
Where bold ideas meet smart capital, thriving ecosystems and growth for startups.

Saturn is a venture ecosystem headquartered in Cape Town, South Africa. Previously trading as W 3 O L A B S, we have invested in and incubated over 140 startups, built one of the continent's leading Web3 communities, and developed programs that have reached founders across Africa and LATAM.

We work with early stage founders, corporates, and institutions. Our leadership has operated at the highest levels of African business and finance, navigating every wave of emerging technology from blockchain to AI, stablecoins, and fintech.
